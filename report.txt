Limitaciones:

Bold and italic:
- Nested formatting of the same type (e.g., **bold **inner** bold**) is not supported to avoid ambiguity.
- Mixing *** with other delimiters (e.g., *italic***) requires explicit spacing (e.g., *italic* **) because *** is tokenized as TRIPLE.

General:
- El input tiene que acabar con una nueva línea. No sé si eso es lo que debería ocurrir o no.
